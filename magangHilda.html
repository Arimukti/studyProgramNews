Student Internship Success at BPS Serang: Hilda Hudaifah Completes Three-Month Internship

Serang – The Mathematics Study Program at Universitas Pamulang (Unpam) Serang Campus proudly announces the success of one of its students, Hilda Hudaifah, in completing an internship program at the Central Statistics Agency (Badan Pusat Statistik - BPS) of Serang Regency. The internship was conducted over a full three-month period, from February 24, 2025, to May 23, 2025.

This internship is a concrete result of the well-established collaboration between the Mathematics Study Program at Unpam Serang Campus and BPS Serang Regency. During the internship, Hilda had the opportunity to apply various mathematical theories and concepts learned in lectures into real-world practice. She was directly involved in various statistical activities and data processing tasks central to BPS’s mission, gaining valuable hands-on experience.

“We are very enthusiastic about Hilda’s success in completing this internship program,” said Hirawati Lubis, S.Pd., M.Si., Head of the Mathematics Study Program. “This proves that graduates of the Mathematics Study Program at Unpam Serang Campus possess relevant competencies and are ready to contribute to the workforce, especially in data and statistics fields. We hope the cooperation with BPS Serang Regency will continue and provide more opportunities for our students to hone their skills.”

This internship experience not only enriched Hilda Hudaifah’s knowledge and skills but also serves as an important bridge between academia and industry. It is hoped that this achievement will inspire other Mathematics Study Program students to actively seek practical experiences to prepare themselves for future challenges.
